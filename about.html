Our Legacy of Excellence

For over a decade, Noble Real Estate has redefined luxury living through unparalleled service, market expertise, and a commitment to our clients' success.

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2025 by Bitandam Joshua Bugagnanbon, Noble Real Estate began as a boutique firm with a vision to transform Ghana's luxury real estate landscape. What started as a small team of passionate professionals has grown into the nation's most trusted name in premium property services.

Our first major success came in 2012 with the landmark sale of the Oceanview Estate in Accra, setting a new benchmark for luxury properties in the region. This achievement established our reputation for handling exclusive, high-value transactions with discretion and expertise.

Our Vision for the Future

As we look ahead, Noble Real Estate is committed to sustainable luxury development and expanding our global network. We're pioneering innovative technologies like virtual property tours and blockchain-based transactions to enhance our clients' experience.
